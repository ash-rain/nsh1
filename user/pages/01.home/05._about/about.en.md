<p class="about__text">NetShell is a Bulgarian software development company specializing in web applications, IoT systems, and enterprise solutions. Founded in 2014 in the heart of the Rose Valley, we combine deep technical expertise with a passion for clean, maintainable code.</p>

<p class="about__text">Our team works across the full technology stack — from PHP and JavaScript on the frontend to C# and .NET on the backend, with hands-on experience in hardware integration, RFID systems, and embedded devices. We serve clients ranging from local businesses to international projects.</p>

<p class="about__text">As active contributors to the open source community, our packages have been downloaded by thousands of developers worldwide. We believe in transparency, quality, and building software that lasts.</p>
